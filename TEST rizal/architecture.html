Casa Manila — Architecture Studio

An academic architectural study inspired by the colonial bahay-na-bato residence now known as Casa Manila.

Project Abstract

This studio explores Casa Manila’s spatial logic, façade composition, and material language— adobe, wood, capiz, and wrought iron—through measured drawings and visual analysis. The goal is to communicate how form, climate, and culture intersect in the bahay-na-bato typology.

At a Glance

  • Typology: Bahay-na-Bato
  • Location: Intramuros, Manila
  • Focus: Plans • Elevations • Details • Proportions
